Disabilies and Solutions in IT


Assistive technologies are designed to compensate people with disabilities.
There is the aim to be as a 'normal' individual or even be somewhat extra human, where possible. These technologies, can and do greatly improve the lives of people around the world. But their distribution is often down to the GDP or wealth of the country. Often the poorest countries with the most near are somewhat lacking sufficient support.
