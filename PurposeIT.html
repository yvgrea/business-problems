Purpose


The purpose of IT in Business will depend on the sector being explored, but there are several technologies common to all businesses;

Digital Accounting
Digitial Marketing
Digital Data security
Digital Legal compliance
Digital People management
Digital Data Analytics
Digital Data distribution
Web connectivity
Network Infrastructure


The purpose of IT in modern business is about managing the accuracy of the data processing and keeping up to date with the processing so as to remain competitive and viable as a business.
Money
All commercial businesses need accurate money management software like sage, software that automates the calculations of amounts essential for a business to remain legal. Accounting software calculates values that if carried out by a human would be prone to error, automation through computing software and hardware, greatly improves accuracy and the allows companies to scale the management of their data.
Marketing
Online marketing is essential for reaching a wider audience. The use of responsive code features enables targetted campaigning. IT allows the analysis and targetting of campaigns to the desired audience.
Communication
Modern companies require effect communication channels, the company website and other web based resources will serve as a 365 days a year communication tool to reach anyone who has internet access.
